Review date: 2026-07-12

This is a practical pre-release review, not a legal opinion or trademark clearance.

## Observed neighboring designs

Searches returned many generic music-themed cows with headphones. Most use black-and-white spots, pink snouts, animal ears, large round eyes, conventional hooves, dancing poses, musical notes, or polished flat-vector rendering.

No reviewed result matched 小K's complete fixed combination:

- soft, heavy, forward-leaning rounded cow body with merged head and torso
- one long white horizontal eye window with two drooping black eyes
- two short curved horns and no animal ears
- oversized warm-yellow headphones
- integrated minimal muzzle
- clock-shaped tail
- spring-like legs with solid black short boots
- rough monochrome editorial crayon style

## Risk controls

- Preserve the horizontal eye window, clock tail and spring legs as the strongest differentiators.
- Avoid black-and-white cow spots, pink protruding snouts, conventional hooves, musical notes and dancing-cow poses.
- Do not market 小K as a parody, skin or derivative of another mascot.
- Keep source assets, prompts, human-directed revisions and attribution records.
- Repeat image and trademark searches before merchandise, registration or a major commercial launch.

## Result

No obvious exact visual match was identified in this limited search. Generic overlap remains because cows and headphones are common mascot ingredients. Release readiness depends on the complete combination, independent source assets and clear attribution rather than exclusive ownership of generic ideas.
